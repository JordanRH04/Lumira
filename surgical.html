02 — Surgical

Surgical dermatology

Precise removal, minimal scarring, and the highest cure rate — surgical skin care delivered with a steady, experienced hand.

When skin cancer or a growth needs to come off, precision is everything. Our surgical care is built around removing exactly what needs removing — and nothing more — for the cleanest possible result.

What we do

Procedures we perform

Mohs micrographic surgery

The gold standard for many skin cancers — the highest cure rate with the smallest possible defect.

Surgical excision

Complete removal of cancerous and atypical lesions, with margins confirmed by pathology.

Skin biopsy

Precise sampling to diagnose suspicious spots quickly and accurately.

Cyst & lipoma removal

Clean removal of benign growths with attention to the final cosmetic result.

Mole removal

Removal of concerning or unwanted moles, with pathology when clinically warranted.

Scar revision

Improving the appearance of surgical and traumatic scars where it matters most.

Why Mohs earns the extra step.

By checking margins in real time, Mohs surgery removes all of the cancer while sparing the maximum amount of healthy tissue — critical on the face, ears, and hands.

It's more work in the moment, and it's the reason the cure rate is as high as it is.

The Mohs process

01

Remove

The visible cancer is removed in a thin layer under local anesthetic.

02

Map

03

Repeat

Only areas still showing cancer are re-treated — healthy skin is spared.

04

Close

Once margins are clear, the site is repaired for the best cosmetic outcome.
