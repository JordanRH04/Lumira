Our practice

People behind the precision.

A small, board-certified team that treats dermatology as a craft — and treats you like a person, not an appointment slot.

Lumira began with a simple frustration.

Skin care had become rushed and impersonal — five-minute visits, no explanation, no follow-through. Dr. Elena Reyes founded Lumira in 2014 to prove dermatology could be both rigorously clinical and genuinely human.

Today the practice spans medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology under one roof, with the same team following your skin health over years — not just a single visit.

What we stand for

Three commitments behind every visit.

Clinical precision

Evidence-based protocols, documented findings, and diagnoses explained in plain language.

Real continuity

The same team follows your skin over time, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Unhurried care

Visits that take the time they need. No rushing, no jargon walls, no guesswork.
